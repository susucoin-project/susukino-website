SUSUCOIN

The cryptocurrency supporting the world's largest anonymous free speech platforms. Susucoin is a proof-of-work cryptocurrency designed with added transactional storage capacity for the purpose of archiving free speech. With its own genesis block, susucoin is an independent blockchain built on wallet software forked from the bitcoin project.
